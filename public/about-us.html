Behind Our Project

Meya's Vogue was created to give users an interactive and enjoyable fashion experience. Our goal is to inspire creativity and self-expression through styling and outfit combinations. Our website name is just a combination of our names, Mischka + Eya = Meya ! We made our project fashion-themed because it is one of our main interests and we wanted to incorporate something that we enjoy into this project so that it isn't purely academic and so that we can also have fun making this website! Meya's Vogue is 2000s themed and is meant to give a nostalgic feeling. Shown beside are some of our inspiration pictures.
